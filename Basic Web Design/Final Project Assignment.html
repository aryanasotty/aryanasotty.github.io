About Us

Cozy Neighborhood Cafe was founded by a group of local friends who wanted to create a welcoming space for the community. We're committed to using locally-sourced and sustainable ingredients in all of our menu items.

Meet our dedicated team of baristas and chefs who bring their passion for great food and coffee to every cup and plate.
